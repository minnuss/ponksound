What we do

Recording

Today, everybody can record at home, but there is a huge difference between home recording and professional recording. Our studio is located in Belgrade, where you can record almost any type of sound. We offer voiceover recording in English for all your needs.

Our Examples

Audio Editing

We edit either ours or your recordings, no matter where are they recorded. Services that we offer include time and pitch correction (vocals, guitars, etc.). We supply full and partially edited vocals depending on your needs.

Our Examples

Mixing

The most important and demanding process for your audio, song, radio ad, jingle, tv spot or podcast. We edit the sound for your product, whether it is steam mixing or mix made of individual elements.

Our Examples

Mastering

If your audio is already well balanced and mixed, but you need a final touch and master for your final audio record, we are here to bring it to a competitive level for all streaming services.

Podcast editing

About

Dragan Ponjević Ponk is Senior Audio Engineer in the media production industry for more than 20 years. He is highly skilled in music production, audio engineering, vocals editing, mixing and mastering, sound design, and radio and podcast production as well.

Location

I live in Belgrade, capitol of Serbia, but work with clients all over the world.

Collaboration & Clients

In his professional career, he has worked with clients from all over the world, but mostly with well-known Balkan artists. Some of them:
Severina, Elitni Odredi, Dj Shone, Maya Berovic, Kija Kockar, Devito, Cvija, Cya, Juice, Leon.


Radio stations from the Balkan region that he have worked with:  TDI Radio, Radio Index, Radio Antena bgd, Radio S, Radio AS FM, WTF Radio, Radio Novosti and many others.

Work

My work is based on long-term trust, with a large number of clients, with whom I have been cooperating for many years to mutual satisfaction.


I am currently the main audio producer of the FM Play media group for the last 5 years.
